Self assessment

Q1 Q2 Q3 Q4 Q5 Q6 Q7 Q8 Q9 Q10 Q11 Q12

Academic

Q1. How well would you rate yourself in writing reports? (grammar, language proficiency, vocabulary etc.)
Q2. Do you think that your ideas are original and concrete?
Q3. Do you think that you are able to research on the right materials and these materials are able to bring value to the project?
Q4. Did you present the concepts clearly and accurately. Did you spoke with confidence, used easy-to-understand language, and were able to fully engage the audience?
Q5. Do you think you are able to weigh pros and cons before making a decision?
Q6. Are you able to finish your task by deadline or even in advance? Or do you rush only at the last minute?

CCA

Q7. Are you attend all the meetings arranged?
Q8. How well are you able to communicate with your peers?
Q9. Do you think you are able to plan activities to minimize the occurrence of possible critical events?

Intern/Job

Q10. How fast do you pick up new technical skills?
Q11. How well are you able to communicate with your peers/ colleagues?
Q12. How stressed do you feel when you are handed a task that you have no prior knowledge of?
