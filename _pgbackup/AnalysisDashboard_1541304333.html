          Technical proficiency

          Demonstrates basic technical skills, needs to improve on learning and comprehensive capabilities

          Demonstrates technical skills, demonstrates adequate learning and comprehensive capabilities

          Consistently demonstrates outstanding technical skills, demonstrates strong learning and comprehensive capabilities

          Interpersonal skills and teamwork

          Displays  complete lack of communication skills.

          Demonstrates limited use of effective communication skills.

          Uses effective communication skills.

          Consistently demonstrates outstanding teamwork skills.

          Adaptability

          Rigid, displays inability to cope with changes, takes a lot of time and effort to adapt to new environment.

          Displays ability to deal with changes, takes time to adapt to new environment.

          Flexible, possesses fast learning skills and can cope with changes in situations within an average amount of time.

          Highly flexible, possesses exceptional learning skills and can cope with a multitude of situations within a short amount of time.

          Language and Expression

          Vague and ambiguous writing with awkward sentence structure. Many grammatical errors that affected meaning in many parts with poor word choice.

          Vague and ambiguous writing with simple sentence structure. Many grammatical errors that affected meaning in some parts with some inaccurate word choice.

          Mostly clear and readable with some varied sentence structure. Some grammatical errors that do not impede meaning of the sentence with good word choice.

          Clear and readable with varied sentence structure. A few minor grammatical errors that do not impede meaning of the sentence with very effective word choice.

          Presentation & Delivery

          Explained concepts moderately well but with some inaccuracies.

           

          Language was moderately easy to understand with poor audience engagement. The slides were adequately designed and formatted. Presentation style and flow can be quite awkward with a few transition errors.

          Explained concepts moderately well  but with some inaccuracies.

           

          Language was generally easy to understand and able to engage audience sufficiently. The slides were adequately designed and formatted. Presentation flow and structure were moderately clear but with a few transition errors.

          Explained concepts adequately well with sufficient accuracy.

           

          Spoke with confidence, used easy-to-understand language with minor pauses and fillers. Able to engage audience sufficiently. The slides were well designed and nicely formatted. Presentation flow and structure were sufficiently clear with some transition awkwardness.   

          Explained concepts clearly and accurately

          Spoke with confidence, used easy-to-understand language, and were able to fully engage the audience. The slides were well designed and nicely formatted. Presentation flow and structure were also clear with smooth transition.

          Research Depth

          Research is of insufficient depth with very few credible sources used

          Research is of moderate depth with few credible sources used

          Adequate research was carried out with a few diverse range of credible sources used

          Thorough background research was carried out. Used a wide range of diverse and credible sources to support argument

          Creativity & Innovation

          No demonstration of ideas generation; ideas generated are complete imitation of what is already available or ideas are too general to see actual value

          Ideas generated demonstrate moderate creativity but may be what is already in the market. Ideas may also be too general without details or refinement

          Uses idea-generating techniques to develop ideas that demonstrate adequate creativity by pushing conventional boundaries. Ideas may use some common ideas in a relatively new way

          Uses idea-generating techniques to develop ideas.

          Ideas demonstrate creativity and ingenuity by going outside of conventional boundaries or use common ideas in a new or surprising way

          Analytical Skill

          Merely copies off information either incompletely or with major inaccuracies

           

          Conclusion is also lacking in depth and insights are inconsistent with evidence presented, presented in a fragmented manner without clear order

          Interprets information inaccurately, incompletely or with major omissions.

           

          Analysis given also lack depth and insights/conclusion may be illogical or inconsistent with the evidence presented

          Interprets information accurately and appropriately with only minor inconsistencies, irrelevancies or omissions

           

          Analysis given are also adequately insightful with good logic and insights consistent with evidence

          Interprets information accurately and appropriately in given context.

           

          Analysis given are also exceptionally insightful, well-supported, logically consistent and complete

          Student fails to prioritizes the task.
